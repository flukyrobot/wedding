Our Story

On November 27, 2012, Jeannine sent a message to a stranger on OkCupid with no profile, only pictures and a strange username.

They exchanged messages back and forth through the website for a few days, until Jeannine finally got tired of draining her phone battery and gave him her number (in other words, she manned up!). It was only two or three days later that Paul suggested that they hang out. Jeannine already had a date planned with someone else whom she had been talking to for awhile, but Paul wanted to meet her first. Her first date was to take place Saturday evening, so Paul talked her into meeting him Friday night instead. Though Jeannine was taken back by his leather jacket and strange facial hair at first, the date ended up being a huge success.

They went out again two days later, the date they had originally planned, but added Jeannine’s younger sister, since the pressure was off. They were inseparable from that day on. Shortly after they would become “Facebook official,” which is something the kids do nowadays, and decided to move in together a mere three months later.

In June of 2013, Jeannine and Paul went on their first vacation together to Punta Cana, Dominican Republic with two of Paul’s friends. There they celebrated their six month anniversary by getting a spectacular case of sun poisoning each.

When they returned, they decided to adopt two kittens to fill the void left by Jeannine’s cat, who had passed prior to taking their vacation. On June 24th, Dory and Mady were added to their little family. While some people feel that everything happened too fast, the timing is different for everyone. Paul knew the time was right and decided that he didn’t want Jeannine to reactivate her OkCupid account and find that mystery guy she had gone out with in between their first and second dates; he put a ring around Dory’s collar and asked Jeannine to marry him on July 7, 2013. She said yes, obviously. After months of searching and looking at over 50 homes, on Valentine’s Day 2014, Paul and Jeannine closed on their first house and moved in on March 1st.

The rest of our story involves all of you… help us get it off to a wonderful start by joining us for our wedding.
